Conversation inspector
Clicking on a comment link (for example @1) adds the comment to this conversation inspector. Clicking on comment links of comments that are already in the conversation inspector allows you to dive deeper into a conversation.

This conversation inspector is meant as a proof of concept for busy comment sections of websites like reddit and youtube. The problem it tries to solve is the case where you have a very busy comment section and the comments arent ordered chronologically but instead by upvotes or relevance. The problems you get are that:

  1. When people start replying to each others comments the conversation can get lost because of the none chronological ordering of the comments.
  2. When a conversation is long and you use a "view replies" button to expand it, the comments become very deeply nested (this happens alot on reddit).
  3. Reddit and Youtube use a system where you can only reply to a single comment. This system allows people to reply/address multiple comments at once.
